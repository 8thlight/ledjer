!1 Deposits

The ledger can accept deposits. Deposits increase the balance of the ledger.

|script|ledger statement generator|

!|Deposits|
|amount|balance?|
|100|100|
|200|300|

A statement for a ledger displays each deposit on a separate line. The total is shown on the last line of the statement.

|Query:Statement|
|lineitem|
|Deposit: $1.00|
|Deposit: $2.00|
|Total: $3.00|
