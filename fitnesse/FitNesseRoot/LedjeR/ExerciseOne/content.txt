Ledgers can accept deposits. The balance of the ledger must be equal to the sum of all deposits.

|script|ledger statement generator|0|

!|Deposits|
|amount|balance?|
|100|100|
|200|300|

A statement for a ledger displays each deposit on a separate line. The total is shown on the last line of the statement.

|script|
|check|generate statement|Deposit $1.00\nDeposit $2.00\nTotal $3.00|
