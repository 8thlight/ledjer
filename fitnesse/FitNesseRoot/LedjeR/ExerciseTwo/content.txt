The ledger can accept payments as well as deposits. Payments are negative values that decrease the balance of the ledger. 

|script|ledger statement generator|

!|Deposits|
|amount|
|10000|

!|Payments|
|amount|payee|balance?|
|100|Apple|9900|
|200|Amazon|9700|
|300|BBC|9400|




A statement for a ledger displays each payment and deposit on a separate line. The total is shown on the last line of the statement.

|script|
|check|generate statement|Deposit $100.00\nPayment to Apple $1.00\nPayment to Amazon $2.00\nPayment to BBC $3.00\nTotal $95.00|
